How PawPair Works

PawPair is designed to remove uncertainty from pet care by combining smart matching, verified caregivers, and seamless booking all in one trusted platform.

For Pet Owners

1. Create Your Pet Profile

Share your pet’s breed, age, temperament, medical needs, feeding routine, and personality traits to help us find the perfect caregiver match.

2. Discover Trusted Caregivers

Browse verified caregivers with detailed bios, certifications, real reviews, availability calendars, and transparent pricing.

3. Secure Booking & Messaging

Chat safely within the platform, confirm availability, and book services using secure, protected payments.

4. Real-Time Updates

Receive live photo updates, activity logs, and check-ins so you always know how your pet is doing.

For Caregivers

1. Apply & Get Verified

Caregivers complete identity verification, background checks, and experience validation to ensure platform safety.

2. Set Your Services

Choose the services you offer — dog walking, sitting, boarding, grooming, or training — and set your availability and rates.

3. Get Matched & Paid

Receive booking requests from compatible pet owners and get paid securely after successful service completion.

4. Build Your Reputation

Earn reviews, ratings, and badges that increase trust and visibility on the platform.

Trust & Safety First
